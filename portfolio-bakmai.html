Bak Mai Application

On demand fuel delivery application.

Bak Mai is a Malaysia-based worldwide online gasoline delivery marketplace. Fun fact: in some parts of Malaysia, they're called as Fuel Panda . They are, at their core, a gasoline delivery service that connects the petrol stations in question with the individuals who need it the most! Indeed, it's a basic concept that has shown to be quite effective. Its main purpose is to allow customers to request gasoline via a website or mobile application.

Bak Mai's technique of ordering is a step-by-step process. The personnel at Bak Mai begin by gathering the customer's postal information. The data is then filtered depending on the information entered by consumers, such as the kind of gasoline and the amount of fuel. Then, depending on the GPS of the customer's position, Bak Mai's staff will purchase gasoline from a nearby gas station and transport it to the customer's location.
