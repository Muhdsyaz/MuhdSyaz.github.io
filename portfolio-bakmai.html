Bak Mai Application

On demand fuel delivery application.

Bak Mai is a Malaysia-based online gasoline delivery marketplace known as Fuel Panda in some parts of Malaysia. They connect petrol stations with customers in need of fuel. Customers can request gasoline through their website or app. Bak Mai follows a step-by-step process, starting with gathering customer's postal information. They filter the data based on customer preferences like fuel type and quantity. Using GPS, Bak Mai purchases gasoline from nearby gas stations and delivers it to the customer's location.
